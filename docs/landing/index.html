What it does

One app. Your whole phone, on the desktop.

Native on both sides — Material 3 on Android, AppKit on macOS. No browser, no relay, no middleman.

Screen mirror

See your phone. Control it from your Mac.

A live, low-latency stream of your phone's screen — click and type to drive it. Or flip it around: put your Mac's screen on the phone, or use the phone as a real second display.

Up to 120 FPS HEVC (H.265) Interactive control Second display
Files

Your phone's storage, in a Finder-style browser.

Walk through DCIM, Downloads, Music — every folder, with live file counts and sizes. Pull files onto your Mac, or drop files from your Mac straight into any folder on the phone.

Full file tree Folder stats Two-way transfer Image thumbnails
Messages

Read and reply to texts without reaching for your phone.

Your full SMS history, in a clean conversation view. Read the codes, answer the threads, send replies from your keyboard — contact names resolved automatically.

Full SMS history Send from Mac Contact names
Send & Quick Drop

Move files and clipboard in a single gesture.

Drag onto the window, or hit a global hotkey to summon Quick Drop from anywhere. Push your clipboard with one click. Incoming files land neatly in ~/Downloads/AirBridge.

Drag & drop Global hotkey Send clipboard SHA-256 verified
And more

Everything else it quietly handles.

Device dashboard

Battery, free storage and RAM at a glance the moment you connect — plus your wallpaper, Phone Link style.

Clipboard sync

Copy here, paste there — text and rich content, both directions.

Send to Mac

Select text in any Android app and push it straight to your Mac.

Photo gallery

Browse the whole photo library and pull originals at full resolution.

QR pairing

Scan once. Keys exchanged, devices trusted — no accounts.

Auto-discovery

Bonjour / mDNS finds the pair on your network. Zero IP typing.

HEVC pipeline

Efficient H.265 — crisp picture, low latency, light on Wi-Fi.

Menu bar & login

Always-on status in the menu bar and optional launch at login.

Speaks your language

Localized in every EU language — the interface follows your system language automatically, on both the phone and the Mac.

Privacy by design

Stays on your Wi-Fi. Full stop.

No relay servers, no analytics, no account to leak. AirBridge talks directly between your two devices and never phones home.

Direct on your LAN

Traffic never leaves your local network. No internet required.

Ed25519 device keys

Each device signs every reconnection. Pairing is cryptographic, not a password.

SHA-256 transfers

Every file is checksum-verified end to end. What you send is what arrives.

Zero telemetry

No tracking, no analytics, no cloud. Your data is nobody's business but yours.

Setup in a minute

Three steps, then forget about it.

01

Scan the QR

Open both apps and scan the code once. Public keys are exchanged and the two devices trust each other.

02

They find each other

Bonjour discovery links them automatically whenever they're on the same Wi-Fi. No IP addresses, ever.

03

Use everything

Mirror, files, messages, transfers, clipboard. It reconnects on its own each time you come home.
